# Northwind Business & Sales Analysis

End-to-end data analysis project based on the Northwind database, focusing on sales performance, employee profitability, and business optimization.

---

## Project Overview
This project analyzes sales, products, customers, and employees using the Northwind sample database.
The goal is to extract actionable business insights that improve profitability and decision-making.

---

## Objectives
- Identify top-performing employees by total profit
- Analyze the impact of discounts on profitability
- Evaluate performance across product categories
- Detect strengths, gaps, and optimization opportunities
- Translate data insights into business recommendations

---

## Tools & Technologies
- SQL – data extraction and analysis  
- Python – Pandas, NumPy, data exploration  
- Tableau – dashboards and visual analysis  
- Excel – raw data handling  
- PowerPoint – business presentation  

---

## Data Source
Northwind sample database (orders, order details, products, customers, employees).

---


---

## Key Insights
- Top employees achieve high profitability without excessive discounting
- Certain product categories generate strong margins but are underutilized
- Discount-heavy strategies do not necessarily correlate with higher profit
- Clear opportunities for sales optimization and employee training

---

## Business Recommendations
- Focus on high-margin product categories
- Encourage pricing discipline among sales staff
- Use employee-level performance metrics for targeted improvement
- Align discount strategy with profitability, not volume alone
